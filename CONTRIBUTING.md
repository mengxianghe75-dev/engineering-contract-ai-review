# Contributing

This repository is primarily a source-available product demo and personal portfolio project.

External contributions may be considered, but there is no promise that feature requests, issues, or pull requests will be accepted or maintained.

By submitting code, documentation, examples, or other materials, you confirm that:

- You have the right to submit the contribution.
- Your contribution can be distributed under the same noncommercial license used by this project.
- The author may also use, modify, distribute, and commercially license the contribution as part of this project or related commercial versions.

Please do not submit real contracts, customer data, secrets, credentials, or confidential business materials.
